Recent Stars

Stephen Curry

Steph Curry is currently the point guard for the Golden State Warriors. He has been in the NBA for 6 years total. He was elected to the NBA in 2009 but had to rest for half a year in 2011 because of a ankle injury. He returned to the NBA in 2012 and continued until now. He is a 4 year all-star and the 2015 MVP. In 2015, he led the Golden State Warriors to win the champions in 40 years. Steph curry is best known for his three-point shots. With a average three-point accuracy of 44.3%, he is the best in the league.

Lebron James

Lebron James is currently the power forward of the Cleveland Cavaliers. He ha been in the NBA for 13 years total. He was eleccted into NBA in 2003 and played in the Cleveland Cavaliers for 7 years then moved to Miami Heat in 2011. He played there and helped them get 2 championships and left after 2014, back to the Cavaliers. Lebron James can be simply described as the heart of a team. He has the leadership and the skills to help a team from not being able to make the playoffs to get in the chapionships. We were able see that when he moved to the Cavaliers.

Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant is the small forward of the Oklahoma City Thunders. He has been in the NBA for 9 years total. He was elected in 2007 to the Seattle Sonics, but the team moved to Oklahoma and became the Thunders. During the 2013-14 season, he was able to get MVP but not able to get the championships. Like Lebron James, he is the heart of a team. He not only can perform really well himself but also has good leadership and expirence. In the 2015-16 season, he got a hamstring injury, resulting a 6 game rest. During that period, his team did not perform as well when he was there. When he came back, the team started there winning streaks again.

James Harden

James Harden is the shooting guard of the Houston Rockets. He has been in the NBA for 7 years. He was elected in 2009 to the Oklahoma City Thunders and played there for three years, then was traded into the Houston Rockets and he played there ever since. In the Houston Rockets, he was able to earn the recognition of one of the best shooters in the NBA. In 2015, he got the National Basketball Players Association's MVP. He is famously known for his rhythm when dribbling. James Harden has a different rhythm than all of the rest of the guards. Guarding him is often a really hard job to do.
